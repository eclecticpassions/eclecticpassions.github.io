Content Expectations

This blog is a little outlet for me to document my tech explorations and topics I find interesting. Posts are grouped into /series for easier access and have /tags for suggesting related content.

Quick disclaimer: All content you read on BurgeonLab.com is created by a human and will never be fully generated by GenAI. Learn what AI tools I do use on this blog.

Soon, I will be taking up the #100DaysToOffload challenge to help incentivize me to become a sustainable blogger. Topics might become more diverse, so consider subscribing with RSS1 to stay updated on future content (I appreciate your readership!). For now, BurgeonLab will mainly be a tech-focused blog.

Origin of My Passion for Technology

Ever since I was a kid, I loved computers and all things technology. I still remember my mom getting me my first computer—a HP Pavilion tower PC with Windows 95. I was lucky to get early exposure to a second OS at school too, with the iMac G3. Oh, days were much simpler back then…
